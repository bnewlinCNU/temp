Cool Dachshund Facts:

  • Dachshunds were origninally bred to hunt Badgers!

    • "Dachshund" is actually german for "Badger Dog", because their short legs would help them track scents into tight holes other dogs could not

  • Dachshunds come in various coat lengths and styles!

  • For a brief time, Dachshunds were called "Badger Dogs"!

    • This was during WWII, where any association with Germany was taboo in the US.

    This led to a momentary rebranding of the dogs, which obviously didn't stick around long
